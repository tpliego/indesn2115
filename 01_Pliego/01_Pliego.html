Beef Taco Casserole


Ingredients

  • 1 lb of ground beef, preferably 80% lean
  • 1 large bag of tortillas chips
  • 3 cups of refried beans
  • 2 cups of black beans
  • 2 cups of sweet corn
  • 1 large tomato, diced
  • 2 bags of Shredded Mexican Style Four Cheese, 8oz
  • 2 cups of mild salsa, smooth
  • 1 head of Iceburg lettuce, chopped
  • 1 bag of taco seasoning mix, 2oz
  • 1 container of Sour Cream, 16oz
  • 1 cup of black olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1 bunch of cilantro, coarsely chopped

Materials

  • 8x16 casserole pan, clear
  • Baking spray
  • Chef Pans
  • Mixing bowls

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Brown ground beef and dump out excess oil. Add taco seasoning. Mix until seasoning is evenly spread.
  3. In large mixing bowl add the ground beef, black beans, sweet corn, diced tomato, sliced olives, cilantro and 1 bag of Four cheese. Set aside any leftovers veggies for later.
  4. Using Chef pan, cook refried beans, add in salsa until velvety smooth. Combine finished beans to large mixing bowl and stir all together.
  5. Using the bag itself, crush tortilla chips until pieces are relatively bite-size. Add into large mixing bowl.
  6. Spray casserole pan, add in large mixing bowl, spread out until even. After mix is laid out evenly, add a smooth coat of sour cream, and sprinkle bag of cheese ontop.
  7. Cook in oven, set to 350, for 20 mins or until cheese is hot and bubbly. When finished, let casserole cool for 10 minutes.
  8. Serve with Iceburg lettuce and leftover veggies on top.
